Kai.Mar
Type 1 Diabetes · Insulin Pump User
Always have a quick-acting sugar source in Kailee's backpack at all times.
If Kailee's blood sugar is below 70, do not leave Kailee alone. Treat onsite. An adult should accompany her at all times.
Symptoms of High Blood Sugar
Kailee may be very thirsty and urinate frequently per Mom.
1
If Kailee is suspected to have a low blood sugar in class, test her blood sugar and give juice/tablets before going to the health office. An adult should accompany her at all times.
2
Give 15 grams of quick-acting sugar — 4 oz juice, or 3 glucose tabs, or 2 pks Smarties — with NO BOLUS.
3
Retest blood sugar in 10 minutes. If still below 70, repeat juice or tablets. If hypoglycemic 3 times, call parent.
4
Once blood sugar is above 70: provide 15 grams of carbohydrate AND protein snack (e.g., peanut butter/crackers) with NO BOLUS, if next meal is not scheduled within 1 hour.
Kailee should remain in adult supervision until blood sugar is above 70.
